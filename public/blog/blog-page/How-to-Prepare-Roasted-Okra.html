How to Prepare Roasted Okra

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound okra
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Method:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Wash okra with cool water and completely pat dry with towels.
  • Toss okra with olive oil, salt and pepper on rimmed baking sheet. Spread out to avoid steaming. Roast for 20 minutes, until okra is golden brown, shaking pan halfway through.
