How to Make Overnight Oats with Maple and Caramel

Ingredients for one serving:

  • 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup almond milk

For breakfast toppings:

  • Almond butter
  • Banana
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Flaxseed meal

For snack/dessert toppings:

  • Granny smith apples
  • Marzetti Classic Caramel Dip
  • Toasted pecans
  • Cocoa nibs

Method:

  1. Combine oats, chia seeds and maple syrup in single serving jar and stir. Pour in almond milk and stir until combined. Press oats down into milk to ensure they are submerged.
  2. Store covered in refrigerator overnight to set.
  3. Garnish with toppings of choice and serve cold.
